# Supply Chain Cost & Operational Efficiency Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes a dataset collected from a mid-size Fashion and Beauty startup to find ways to optimize costs and operational efficiency of the brand.

## Business Questions

* Where is money being lost to inefficient shipping?
* Which suppliers are negatively affecting operational efficiency?
* Are we holding the right inventory for the right products?

**Find ways in which the supply chain is performing inefficiently and recommend solutions on how to optimize costs.**

## Dataset

Dataset used: [Supply Chain DataSet](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/amirmotefaker/supply-chain-dataset) published by [Amir Motefaker](https://www.kaggle.com/amirmotefaker) on [kaggle.com](https://www.kaggle.com/)

## Key Findings

* Carrier C Is Structurally Inefficient
* Supplier 4 Is a Compounded Operational and Financial Risk
* Working Capital Is Trapped in Overstocked SKUs

## Recommendations

* Review Carrier C SKUs
* Supplier 4 Performance Review
* Reduce Top 10 Overstock SKUs
